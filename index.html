Anti Rug Token $ART is a Solana-based cryptocurrency created to stand in solidarity with victims of rug pulls. Our mission is to rebuild trust and security in the blockchain community.

Token Swap Insurance

Concept Overview

The insurance system acts as a safety net for users investing in untested or new projects by offering partial reimbursement in the event of a rug pull. ART holders can stake their tokens to participate in the insurance pool, and insured users pay premiums in ART tokens for coverage.

Key Features

  • Insurance Coverage: Protects a percentage of the userâ€™s swap amount (e.g., 70-80%). Covers tokens swapped on decentralized exchanges like Raydium or Serum.
  • Premium Payments: Users pay premiums in ART tokens. Premium rates vary based on risk.
  • Community-Managed Pool: Premiums are collected in a decentralized pool funded by ART stakers and used for payouts.
  • Rug Pull Verification: A decentralized panel verifies rug pulls before payouts.

How It Works

  1. User Buys Insurance: Users pay premiums in ART tokens for coverage while swapping tokens.
  2. Stakeholders Fund the Pool: ART holders stake tokens to earn a portion of the premiums.
  3. Community Verification: A decentralized panel verifies claims using tools like Solscan.
  4. Payout: Verified claims are reimbursed from the insurance pool.
