Token Swap Insurance for Anti Rug Token ($ART)

Token Swap Insurance provides a layer of security for investors when swapping or investing in new tokens, protecting them from potential rug pulls. Here's how it works:

  • Insurance Coverage: Protects a percentage of the user's swap amount (e.g., 70-80%).
  • Premium Payments: Users pay premiums in ART tokens for coverage.
  • Community-Managed Pool: ART holders stake tokens to fund the insurance pool.
  • Rug Pull Verification: Community verifies rug pulls before payouts.
