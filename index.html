Anti Rug Token $ART

Anti Rug Token $ART is a Solana-based cryptocurrency created to stand in solidarity with victims of rug pulls. Our mission is to rebuild trust and security in the blockchain community.

Token Swap Insurance

Concept Overview

The insurance system acts as a safety net for users investing in untested or new projects by offering partial reimbursement in the event of a rug pull. ART holders can stake their tokens to participate in the insurance pool, and insured users pay premiums in ART tokens for coverage.
